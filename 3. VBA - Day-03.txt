Loop : is iterator or repeatio of statement/command
================
Example:
	1 2 3 ..100
	100 ...  1
	
Fundamental of loop:
	-init 	/ start from	: 1		100
	-condition/limit	: 100		1
	-steps/incrementer	: +1		-1


There are following types of loop:
	- while loop	
	- for loop
	- do while 
	- do until 
	- foreach
